AFRICAN MANCALA BOARD GAMES

The mancala games are a family of two-player turn-based strategy board games played with small stones, beans, or seeds and rows of holes or pits in the earth, a board or other playing surface. The objective is usually to capture all or some set of the opponent's pieces.

It is among the oldest known games originating from Africa widely played today. The game is fun and easy to learn, yet challenging enough to keep the best board game pros engaged. In keeping with the ancient tradition, Africa Heartwood Project offers these board games in styles and designs from Africa. Each board games is hand-carved.
